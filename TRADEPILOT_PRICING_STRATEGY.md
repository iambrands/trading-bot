## Pricing Psychology

### Why $89/month?

1. **Competitive Positioning**
   - 10% below 3Commas Pro ($99/month)
   - 45% below Cryptohopper Warrior ($162/month)
   - 40% below Bitsgap Pro ($149/month)
   - **Sweet spot** between mid-tier and Pro-tier pricing

2. **Value Perception**
   - Reflects Pro-tier feature parity ($99-$149/month competitors)
   - Includes unique features (AI Analysis, Trade Journal) worth $45-65/month extra
   - Premium positioning without premium pricing
   - Justified by superior education and unique features

3. **Market Analysis**
   - Feature parity with $99-$149/month Pro tiers
   - Unique features not available elsewhere (+$45-65/month value)
   - Still 10-45% below most Pro-tier competitors
   - Maximizes revenue at $155,952 ARR vs. $100,890 at $59/month

4. **Annual Discount**
   - 17% discount = 2 months free
   - Effective monthly cost: $74.17/month
   - Encourages annual commitment
   - Improves cash flow and retention

### Why Single Price?

1. **Simplicity** - No decision paralysis
2. **Clarity** - All features included
3. **Value** - Maximum features for price
4. **Trust** - No hidden fees or limitations
5. **Differentiation** - Stands out from complex tiered competitors
